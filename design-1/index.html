The Method

Four series. One practice.

01

Primal

A functional bodyweight strength practice, balancing push and pull. Where everyone starts.

02

Moves

Progressive strength and mobility, building real capacity in the spine, shoulders, hips, and hands.

03

Progressions

Intermediate skill work — dynamic-to-static holds, hand-supported transitions. For movers ready to go further.

04

Handstand

Complex sequences and inversions. Only achievable once the foundation is solid.
