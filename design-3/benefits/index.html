Why It Works

Five ways this practice changes how you move.

01

Motor Control

Quadrupedal patterns retrain the nervous system's coordination between limbs, improving balance and reaction speed in everyday movement.

02

Structural Efficiency

Balanced push-pull work across the anterior and posterior chains corrects compensation patterns, reducing strain on joints and the spine.

03

Fitness

A full-body conditioning effect — strength, mobility, and cardiovascular capacity trained together rather than in isolation.

04

Sauna

On-site heat therapy supports recovery, circulation, and the post-practice wind-down.

05

Ice Bath

Cold immersion on-site — a contrast practice for recovery, resilience, and mental focus.
