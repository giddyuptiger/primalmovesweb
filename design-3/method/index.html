Our Method

Objectives

Stability, strength, mobility — through quadrupedal movement.

Primal Moves™ trains stability, strength, mobility, and excellent muscle tone through quadrupedal movements and dynamic maneuvers that replicate the locomotive characteristics of animals. Practicing crawling, walking, running, jumping, and climbing in combination builds coordination, balance, and functional capacity.

The Primal Method Is Divided Into Four Series
01

Primal

A functional bodyweight strength practice designed to balance the anterior and posterior chains through a push-pull practice. Where everyone starts.

02

Moves

Progressive strength and mobility — expands movement capacity while developing structural balance and joint stability in the spine, shoulders, hips, and hands.

03

Progressions

Intermediate skill sets, refining your ability to move from dynamic to static holds and introducing dynamic, hand-supported transitions. For movers ready to go further.

04

Handstand

Skill-set refinement, complex movement sequences, and dynamic inversions — only achievable with a solid foundation in the first three series.

Founder

"Think less, move more, breathe."

Nick Brewer founded Primal Moves in Ibiza in 2016, after decades of training across movement modalities. His mission: transmit his experience of movement practice into the lives of many — a system accessible to all, while still physically challenging and skill-building.
