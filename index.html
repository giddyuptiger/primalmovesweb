Primal Moves Venice is a "one and only" place. Located in a quiet part of Venice, walking distance from the boardwalk, it's an easy place to relax, connect with the community, and get your workout in. Primal Moves Venice is not just a gym — it's also a community hub hosting events, parties, and spreading joy through the love of sauna and movement.

Our unique energy is curated by a remarkable team of professional acrobats, stuntwomen, somatic practitioners, and "burners" alike, each bringing a distinctive blend of expertise and passion. This diverse staff infuses the space with an unparalleled vibrancy, creating an atmosphere that inspires growth, encourages authentic expression, and embodies the very best of California's holistic and free-spirited ethos.

The essence of Primal Moves Venice lies in its dynamic and supportive community, fostering an environment where well-being extends beyond physical exertion. Here, individuals gather not only for their workouts but also to forge genuine connections at community events, lively parties, and through the shared joy of sauna and movement.

Our Method

The Primal Moves™ movement practice.

The main focus of Primal Moves™ movement training is to develop stability, strength, mobility, and excellent muscle tone through quadrupedal movements and dynamic physical maneuvers that replicate the locomotive characteristics of animals. By practicing crawling, walking, running, jumping, and climbing in various combinations and intensities, you develop better coordination, balance, and overall functional capacity.

Series 1

Primal

A functional bodyweight strength practice designed to balance the anterior and posterior chains through a push-pull practice.

Series 2

Moves

Progressive strength and mobility — expands movement capacity while developing structural balance and joint stability in the spine, shoulders, hips, and hands.

Series 3

Progressions

Intermediate skill sets focused on refining your ability to move from dynamic to static holds, introducing dynamic transitions and hand-supported movement.

Series 4

Handstand

Skill-set refinement, complex movement sequences, and dynamic inversions — only achievable with a solid foundation.

Benefits

Mobility, stability, strength & community.

Bodyweight training such as Primal Moves™ offers benefits not experienced in a modern-day gym workout — plus post-training recovery in a state-of-the-art ice bath and sauna to speed recovery and reduce inflammation.

Motor control

Emphasizes contra-lateralization, addressing left-right imbalances and facilitating neurological efficiency, pattern recall, and enhanced body awareness.

Structural efficiency

Quadrupedal movements evenly load joints, release tissue stiffness, correct posture, and enhance muscle strength and flexibility.

Fitness level

Improvements in cardiovascular fitness, flexibility, muscular strength, endurance, and body composition within 3 months of regular 2–3x/week practice.

Sauna — circulation & recovery

Heat dilates blood vessels, improving circulation and delivering oxygen and nutrients to muscles, while relaxing tension after training.

Sauna — stress relief

Helps lower stress levels and promotes an overall sense of calm and tranquility.

Ice bath — mindset & recovery

Cold exposure builds mental resilience and confidence, reduces muscle pain, and has an analgesic effect that eases post-workout discomfort.
